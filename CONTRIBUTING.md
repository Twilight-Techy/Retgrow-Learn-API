# Contributing to Retgrow-Learn

Thanks to the Organization's developers for helping improve the Retgrow-Learn platform!

### 1. Source Control Rules
All contributions must be made via Pull Requests targeting the `main` branch. Direct pushes to `main` are strictly forbidden and will be rejected by repository protection rules.

### 2. Intellectual Property
By submitting a Pull Request, code, or any form of improvement ("Contribution") to this repository, you acknowledge and agree that:
- Your contributions are considered "works made for the Platform."
- All rights, title, and interest in your Contributions are irreversibly assigned to the Platform Owner.
- Contributions do not entitle the Organization or the individual developer to any co-ownership, equity, or claim over this underlying codebase or resultant platform.

### 3. File Protection
Do not attempt to modify the `LICENSE` or `CONTRIBUTING.md` files. Any PRs touching these documents must be explicitly approved by the repository owner (`mzone` / the Platform Owner). Unapproved attempts to alter licensing or ownership clauses will result in immediate rejection.
